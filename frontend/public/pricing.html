Frequently asked

What is a credit?

One credit equals roughly 1,000 tokens of hopez-mini input/output. Heavier models consume more credits per call. Exact rates are published in the docs.

Do credits roll over?

Yes — purchased credits never expire as long as your account is active. Free-tier credits reset monthly.

Can I cancel?

Of course. Subscriptions can be canceled at any time from your billing settings; you keep access until the end of the period.

Is there a free tier?

Yes. Every account starts on the Free plan with 100 credits and one API key — enough to evaluate the platform end-to-end.
