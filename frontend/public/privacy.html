What we collect

  • Account data: name, email, password hash.
  • Usage data: API request metadata (model, token counts, timestamps). We do not log prompt or completion content beyond what is required for debugging by request.
  • Billing data: handled by our payment processor — we do not store card numbers.

How we use it

To operate the service, prevent abuse, generate invoices, and improve reliability. We do not sell personal data.

Sharing

API requests are processed by HOPEZ.AI infrastructure under our data policy. Aggregated, non-identifying usage may be used to improve the platform.

Retention

Account data is retained while your account is active and for up to 90 days after deletion to satisfy legal and accounting obligations.

Your rights

You may request export or deletion of your data at any time by emailing privacy@hopez.ai.
